What should you do during this lock down?













Here are some recommendations

  1. Play any online games with friends, or with random people.

  2. Go outside and do some cardio, something like running.

  3. Do an actual workout, you can find many workout videos on youtube.

  4. Play with your pets, or play with siblings, or people in the same household as you, just have some fun!

Of course, some of that is not reasonable for some.
For those who can't do any of those things, sorry, but just go to bed.
